Stage 2

The vault becomes a filesystem contract, not an app feature.

The vault starts being treated as a portable source of truth: Markdown files, schema.yaml, conventions, validation, scripts, and system folders that agents and tools can read directly.

Markdownschema.yamlvalidation
Layer notes

Core idea

Obsidian is an interface; the vault is the product. Structure moves into files, frontmatter, schemas, and scripts that do not depend on a single UI.

What it enabled

Agents, CLIs, scripts, and future tools can operate against the same durable knowledge base.

Limit

Once the vault becomes system infrastructure, routing and safety matter: writes need provenance, validation, and clear boundaries.
